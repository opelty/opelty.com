about opelty

Opelty Software Studio makes software developments and screencasts researching for the perfect balance between UX, design and code optimization. We divide Opelty into 3 small studios, explore them!

OpenSource Studio

Opelty's OpenSource Studio makes open source applications. Those apps are great because the community can help to grow it. Also, all of our developments try to find the perfect balance between design and usability trying to use the best today's development practices. This studio makes totally free applications and publishes those codes on our public GitHub's repository.

Explore More »

Commerce Studio

Opelty's Commerce Studio makes non-open source applications. If you have any idea about an amazing development, we will be happy to build and make your ideas real. Also, all code generated on this studio is owned by the client, so we won't publish it on our public channels like OpenSource studio or ScreenCasts.
Contact us and have your ideas come true!

Contact Us »

ScreenCasts

ScreenCast will be the amazing Opelty's YouTube channel where we will make some useful videos about we what do. Explaining and talking about software development our main idea is allowing you learn more about those subjects.
Contact us if you are interested in a talk or make a tutorial, we will happy to talk with you and come it real.
